Cadescent Aether is a colorful rock-paper-scissors wave 2-player game with energetic rhythme mechanics to give the game a mix of challenge and strategy. Each player must navigate between the three lanes and send out a pair of of three shapes to send out against their foe. Both players have a choice of three shapes; shpere, cube, and triangluar-prism with each shape able defeat the other. The goal of the game is to get as many shapes into your opponsite's side within the time limit.

Cadescent Aether was made during the 2017 Global Game Jam from January 20-22. I worked along side 3 other programmers and took the role as the mechanics developer. I created and polished the rokc-paper-scissors mechanics and player controls. I also ironed out and fine tuned the rules and lane movement mechanics. I also assisted my other team memebers in endvaours such as menu ui and debugging.

Tuber Tyrants is a fun 4 player survivial platform with cartoon visuals and referenctial humour. Each player controls a tuber, each repsresenting a political leader, and try to jump on to the on coming platforms; the surviving tuber is declared the winner. However the game will throw random challenges known as "Alternate Facts" to the game to add difficulty to players and inviting an more intense and exciting experience.

Tuber Tyrants was made during the 2017 PTBO Game Jam from Feburary 21-23. I worked on Tuber Tyrants with 3 other programmers and a artist, I took the role as the mechanics developer, in which I created the controls, platform movement, multi-player and the Alternate Facts; I also assisted in debugging.

The Forgoatten Isles is a first person platformer. Player must traverse through a series of platform puzzles on three islands to find three keys, one on each island, to unlock a grand door and aqcuire the secret pize within. The player can run and jump on different cubes to reach the prize however some cubes behave different and there are hidden traps all over the islands to all new levels of difficulty and challenge to the game.

The Forgoatten Isles was created during the 2017 ToJam from May 5-7. I work in a 5 person team, 3 other programmers and 1 artist. I was the mechanics developer, creating the mechanics for the different blocks, the controls, and hidden traps. I also assisted in level editing and debugging
